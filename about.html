About Islamic Information Centre (An NGO)

Established 2010 | Bengaluru

Established in 2010

Islamic Information Center is a socio-religious organisation dedicated to serving the community.

Our Mission

IIC strives to foster understanding, respect, and harmony through the dissemination of accurate and insightful knowledge about Islam amongst Muslims and Non-Muslims.

Our Vision

To bridge cultural gaps, dispel misconceptions, and promote peaceful coexistence amongst people of all faiths.
